Frequently Asked Questions

How do I count words in my text?
Simply paste or type your text into the text area above. The word counter updates in real time, showing you the total word count, character count, sentence count, and more — no button click needed.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is estimated at 200 words per minute (wpm), which is the average adult silent reading speed. Speaking time uses 130 wpm, typical for presentations and podcasts. Both are rounded up to the nearest minute.
Does this word counter work offline?
Yes. This tool is 100% client-side JavaScript. Once the page loads, it works entirely in your browser with no internet connection required. Your text is never sent to any server.
