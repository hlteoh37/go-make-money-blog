What is an Open Graph image?

An Open Graph (OG) image is the thumbnail that appears when you share a link on social media platforms like Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Slack, and iMessage. The standard size is 1200 × 630 pixels. A compelling OG image dramatically increases click-through rates on shared links.

How to use this social card generator

  • Pick a template that matches your content type — blog post, product launch, tutorial, or announcement.
  • Enter your title, description, author name, and URL.
  • Customize background gradient, text colors, and font sizes using the controls.
  • Click Download PNG to save the 1200×630 image to your device.
  • Upload the image to your CMS or reference it in your <meta property="og:image"> tag.

How to add your OG image to a web page

Once you have downloaded your PNG and hosted it, add these tags inside your <head>:

  • <meta property="og:image" content="https://example.com/your-og-image.png">
  • <meta name="twitter:image" content="https://example.com/your-og-image.png">
  • <meta name="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image">
